## Step 4: Model Stress Test

If the numbers don't work, what would need to change?

- [ ] **Raise prices** — Target higher-value customers or offer more value
- [ ] **Change market** — Target a larger or more accessible market
- [ ] **Change model** — Move from B2C to B2B, or add enterprise tier
- [ ] **Reduce costs** — Find cheaper acquisition channels
